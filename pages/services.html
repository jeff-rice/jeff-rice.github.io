Specialized Techniques

One-handed Style

Master the traditional one-handed approach with focus on consistency, accuracy, and power. I'll help you develop proper wrist position, release timing, and follow-through for maximum effectiveness.

Two-handed Style

Learn the modern two-handed technique that's revolutionizing the sport. I'll teach you the unique footwork, timing, and release mechanics needed to generate powerful rev rates with this style.
