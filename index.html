Welcome to J.Rice Coaching

Welcome to J.Rice Coaching. As a USBC Silver Level coach with a strong focus on youth development, my mission is to help bowlers refine their skills and grow their love for the sport. Bowling is something you can enjoy for a lifetime, and I’m here to support those committed to improving their game and becoming lifelong participants. Whether you’re new to the lanes or looking to reach the next level, I’m ready to guide you in achieving your goals.

A Bit About Me

My love for bowling began when I was a kid, and it’s only deepened over the years. I’ve spent countless hours on the lanes, learning both classic techniques and developing a few unique ones of my own.

My biggest reward, though, is sharing that passion with others. Whether you’re picking up a bowling ball for the first time or looking to refine your skills, I’m here to guide you with personalized coaching every step of the way.

Combining proven techniques with engaging, innovative methods, I aim to make each session as enjoyable as it is effective. For me, bowling isn’t just a game—it’s a way to bring people together and support their growth, and that’s the heart of my approach to coaching.

Ready to elevate your skills and experience the game in a new way? Let’s get started!

Peek Into My Coaching Philosophy

Curious about the approach behind J.Rice Coaching? My focus is on helping you bring out your best, making the experience both effective and enjoyable. It’s not just about the techniques of bowling—it’s about fostering a genuine excitement for the sport with each roll and each pin. Think of it as learning with a coach who’s as enthusiastic about bowling as you are.

My approach is about growth, learning together, and even sharing a few laughs along the way. I believe every bowler’s journey is unique, which makes it all the more rewarding. From beginners to advanced players, I tailor my coaching to your individual style and goals.
